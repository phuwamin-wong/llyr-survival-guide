# Intrapartum fever

## Definition

&#x20;“Intraamniotic infection”&#x20;

ต้องแบ่ง definition ของคนไข้ให้ชัดเจนเพราะมีผลต่อ management

**(1)** 🥵**Isolated maternal fever:** ไข้ 38.0-38.9°C without other signs&#x20;

**(2)** **🦠Suspected intraamniotic infection:**

* ไข้มากกว่าเท่ากับ 39°C or&#x20;
* ไข้ 38.0–38.9°C + one additional clinical risk factor
* fetal tachycardia ≥ 160 > 10 mintues&#x20;
* maternal leukocytosis in the absence of corticosteroids (≥ 15,000 cell/mm3 )
* purulent drainage from the cervical os

**(3)☣️Confirmed intraamniotic infection:** positive amniotic fluid test result (gram stain, glucose level, or culture results consistent with infection) หรือส่อง patho placenta พบ evidence of placental infection/ inflammation.&#x20;

_ACOG 2024_ update เพิ่มเติม “ไม่มีไข้ก็เป็นได้!”&#x20;

The diagnosis of suspected intraamniotic infection may also be made in the absence of maternal fever when other associated clinical signs and symptoms are present.&#x20;

## What to ask back when being Notified?

(ให้นึกถึงทั้ง OBGYN, non OBGYN)

* ไข้สูงกี่ °C? กี่ peak แล้ว? Onset, duration, recent infection?, sick contact
* Localizing signs อื่น ๆ&#x20;
  * non-OB; URI, dysuria/ back pain, diarrhea, GI pain etc.&#x20;
  * OB : ปวดท้อง Ut tenderness, membrane rupture หรือยัง ลักษณะสีกลิ่นน้ำคร่ำ
  * Epidural block ? (เป็น non-infectious fever ได้)
* GA เท่าไหร่ G?P? ครรภ์ก่อนมีปัญหามีไข้อะไรไหม?
* Cx dilatation, Effacement, Station, UC Fetal condition: EFM? FHR? ลูกดิ้นดีไหม?&#x20;

## Patient Evaluation (Signs of Chorioamnionitis)

* Maternal vital signs BT ≥38.0°C&#x20;
* Maternal tachycardia&#x20;
* Fetal tachycardia >160 bpm?
* Uterine tenderness
* AF with purulent cervical discharge/ foul smell
* Maternal Leukocytosis or AF gram stain/culture +ve
* Other localizing sign (rule out non OBGYN cause)

## Initial Management

**(1). 🥵Isolated maternal fever**

* Expectant management ± IV antibiotics for GBS treatment (consider if no other source found)&#x20;
* Antipyretics: paracetamol prn fever
* Notify PED&#x20;
* Continue fetal and maternal monitoring&#x20;

**(2) or (3): 🦠☣️ Suspected and Confirmed intraamniotic infection**&#x20;

* Delivery is indicated (regardless of gestational age)&#x20;
* IV antibiotics for GBS treatment
* Antipyretics: paracetamol prn fever
* Notify PED

## ATB for Chorioamnionitis (III)

ND  : Ampicillin 2 g IV q6h + Gentamicin 240 mg IV OD (1.5 mg/kg IV q8h)

C/S : Ampicillin 2 g IV q6h + Gentamicin 240 mg IV OD (1.5 mg/kg IV q8h) + Metronidazole 500 mg IV single dose or Clindamycin 900 mg IV single dose

Mild Penicillin Allergy: Cefazolin 2 g IV q8h + Gentamicin 240 mg IV OD (1.5 mg/kg IV q8h)

\*\*\*Severe Penicillin Allergy: Gentamicin 240 mg IV OD (1.5 mg/kg IV q8h) + Clindamycin 900 mg IV q8h or Vancomycin 1 g IV q12h

## Intrapratum GBS Prophylaxis Indicated

**Maternal History:**&#x20;

* Previous neonate with invasive GBS disease&#x20;

**Current Pregnancy:** ดู GBS colonization

* Positive Vg/rectal GBS culture at ≥ 36 weeks
* Positive Urine GBS culture at any GA

**Intrapartum (GBS status unknown + any of the following)**

* Preterm birth (GA<37wk)
* PROM > 18 hrs
* Unknown cause of maternal fever
* NAAT positive for GBS
* Known GBS-positive status from previous pregnancy&#x20;

### ATB regimen for GBS prophylaxis

* Ampicillin 2 g IV load then Ampicillin 1 g IV q4-6h until delivery (start when active phase)
* กรณีแพ้ penicillin ⇒ Clindamycin 900 mg IV q8h until delivery
