# Gastrointestinal System

## Maddrey's Discrimination Dunction (MDF) — Alcoholic hepatitis

$$
MDF=4.6\times(PT_{patient}-PT_{control})+Total\space Bilitubin\space(mg/dL)
$$

{% hint style="info" %}
MDF > 32 suggests sever disease; consider steroid
{% endhint %}

## Model for End-Stage Liver Disease (MELD) Score - for assessing liver transplant priority

$$
MELD=3.78\times(\ln(Billirubin)+11.2)\times(\ln(INR)+9.57)\times(\ln(Creatinine)+6.43)
$$

## Serum-Ascites Albumin Gradient (SAAG)

$$
SAAG=Serum\space Albumin-Ascites\space fluid\space Albumin
$$

* SAAG ≥ 1.1 suggests portal hypertension (e.g. Cirrhosis, Heart failure)
* SAAG < 1.1 suggests non-portal causes (e.g. malignant, TB)

## Fibrosis-4 Index (FIB-4 Index)

$$
FIB-4=\frac{Age\space(years)\times AST\space(U/L)}{Platelet\space Count\space(10^9/L)\times ALT\space(U/L)}
$$

* FIB-4 < 1.45 suggests low likelihood of significant fibrosis
* FIB04 > 3.25 suggests High likelihood of significant fibrosis

{% hint style="info" %}
Gray zones exists between these values and may require additional testing like elastography.
{% endhint %}
