## Sodium Correction in Hyperglycemia

{% columns %}
{% column %}
**กรณี Blood sugar < 400 mg%**

$$
SNa = \frac{Glucose-100}{100}\times 1.8
$$
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
**กรณี Blood sugar > 400 mg%**

$$
SNa = \frac{Glucose-100}{100}\times 2.4
$$
{% endcolumn %}
{% endcolumns %}

## Serum Osmolality (Calculated)

$$
Osmolality=2[Na^+]+\frac{Glucose}{18}+\frac{BUN}{2.8}
$$

$$
Osmolar\space gap\space (OG) = Measured\space Osmoles-Calculated\space Osmoles;\space(normal\space \leq10)
$$

## Calcium Correction in Hypoalbuminemia

{% endhint %}

$$
Correct\space Ca^{2+} = Measured\space Ca^{2+} + 0.8\times(4-Serum\space albumin)
