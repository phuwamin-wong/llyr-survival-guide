# Respiratory System

## Alveolar gas equation

### Hypoxemia

$$
P_AO_2 = [FiO_2\times(760-47)]-[\frac{PaCO_2}{R}];where\space R = 0.8
$$

$$
P_AO_2 = 150 - \frac{P_aCO_2}{0.8};\space (FiO_2 = 0.21; \space Room\space air)
$$

### Hypercapnea

$$
P_aCO_2 = \frac{k \times VC_{O_2}}{RR \times VT \times \left(1 - \frac{V_D}{V_T}\right)}
$$

## A-a gradient

$$
A-a\space gradient = P_ACO_2 - P_aO_2
$$

$$
Expected\space A-a\space gradient = 4+\frac{age\space(year)}{4}
$$

## Minute Ventilation

$$
V_E = Tidal\space Volume\space (V_T)\times Respiratory\space Rate\space (RR)
$$

## Tidal Volume

$$
V_T = Alveolar\space space\space (V_A) + Dead\space space\space (V_D)
$$

## Oxygen Delivery (DO<sub>2</sub>)

$$
D_{O_2} = Cardiac\space Output\space (CO)\times Arterial\space Oxygen \space Content \space (C_aO_2)
$$

$$
D_{O_2}=CO\times(1.34\times[Hb]\times S_aO_2 +(0.003\times P_aO_2))
$$

## Pulmonary to Systemic Flow Ratio (Q<sub>p</sub>/Q<sub>s</sub>)

$$
Q_p/Q_s=\frac{S_aO_2-S_vO_2}{P_vO_2-P_aO_2}
$$

## Compliance

$$
C=\frac{\Delta Volume}{\Delta Pressure}
$$

### For static compliance:

$$
C_{static}=\frac{\Delta V_T}{\Delta P_{plateau}-PEEP}
$$

## Airway Resistance (R<sub>aw</sub>)

$$
R_{aw}=\frac{P_{IP}}{Flow}
$$

where P<sub>IP</sub> = Inspiratory Pressure
