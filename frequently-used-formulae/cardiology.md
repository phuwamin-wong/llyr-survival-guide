# Cardiology

## Mean Arterial Pressure (MAP)

$$
MAP = DBP+\frac{Pulse\space Pressure}{3}; where\\Pulse\space Pressure=SBP-DBP
$$

$$
MAP=\frac{SBP +(DBP\times2)}{3}
$$

## Corrected QT Interval (Bazett's Formula)

$$
QT_c=\frac{QT\space (millisecond)}{\sqrt{RR\space (second)}}
$$

## Pulmonary Vascular Resistance

$$
PVR=\frac{Mean\space PAP-PCWP}{CO\times80}
$$

## Coronary Perfusion Pressure

$$
CPP = Diastolic\space BP -LVEDP
$$

## Fraction of V<sub>D</sub> to V<sub>T</sub>

$$
V_D/V_T = \frac{P_aCO_2-P_{expired_{CO2}}}{P_a{CO_2}}
$$
