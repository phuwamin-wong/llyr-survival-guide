# Nephrology

## Glomerular Filtration Rate (GFR), Cockcroft-Gault Equation

$$
CrCl\space(mL/min)=\frac{140-Age\space(year)}{72\times Serum\space Creatinine}\times0.85\space(if\space female)
$$

## Creatinine Clearance using 24-hour urine creatinine

$$
CrCl\space(mL/min)=\frac{Urine\space Creatinine\space (mg/dL)\times Urine\space Volume\space(mL)}{Plasma\space Creatinine\space (mg/dL)\times Time\space (min)}
$$

## Calculated Serum Osmolality

$$
Osmolality=2[Na^+]+\frac{Glucose}{18}+\frac{BUN}{2.8}
$$

## Free Water Deficit

$$
Water\space Deficit=TBW\times(\frac{Serum\space Na^+}{140}-1)
$$

## Total Body Water

$$
Total\space Body\space Water\space (male)= 0.6\times Body\space Weight\space(kg)\\Total\space Body\space Water\space (female)= 0.5\times Body\space Weight\space(kg)
$$

## Fractional Excretion of Sodium (F/E<sub>Na</sub>)

$$
FE_{Na}=\frac{{Urine\space sodium}/{Serum\space sodium}}{{Urine\space creatinine}/{Serum\space creatinine}}\times100
$$

{% hint style="info" %}
Same principle applies to urea, use Urine and Serum urea in place of Urine sodium and Serum sodium
{% endhint %}

## Metabolic acidosis

$$
P_aCO_2=(1.5\times HCO_3^-)+8\pm2
$$

## Serum Anion Gap

$$
Serum\space AG=[Na^+]-([Cl^-]-[HCO_3^-])
$$

## Urine Anion Gap

$$
Urine\space AG = [Na^+]+[K+]-[Cl^-]
$$

## Delta-Delta ratio (for determination of mixed acid-base disorder)

$$
\Delta AG/\Delta HCO_3^-=\frac{AG-12}{24-HCO_3^-}
$$

* 1-2 = pure matabolic acidosis
* < 1 = concurrent with normal AG metabolic acidosis
* \>2 = Concurrent metabolic alkalosis

## Metabolic Alkalosis

$$
\Delta P_aCO_2=0.7\times \Delta HCO_3^-
$$

## Acute Respiratory Acidosis

$$
\Delta HCO_3^-=0.1\times \Delta P_aCO_2
$$

$$
\Delta pH = 0.008\times\Delta  P_aCO_2
$$

## Chronic Respiratory Acidosis

$$
\Delta HCO_3^-=0.4^*\times \Delta P_aCO_2\\Some\space instutution\space use\space 0.35
$$

$$
\Delta pH = 0.003\times\Delta  P_aCO_2
$$

## Acute Respiratory Alkalosis

$$
\Delta HCO_3^- = 0.2\times\Delta P_aCO_2
$$

$$
\Delta pH = 0.008\times\Delta  P_aCO_2
$$

## Chronic Respiratory Alkalosis

$$
\Delta HCO_3^- = 0.5\times\Delta P_aCO_2
$$

## Urine Protein-to-Creatinine Ratio (UPCR)

$$
UPCR\space(g/g)=\frac{Urine\space protein\space(mg/dL)}{Urine\space creatinine\space(mg/dL)}
$$
